Welcome to the Πprocess calculation interface!

Here you can aswer the different questions to calculate the value of the factor Πprocess by selecting one of the four options.

Click the planets at the top to access the different categories.

Click the button "Learn more" to get more information on the question (FIDES reference, recommendation, weight).

The indications MAX, |, MIN and FXD indicate the recommended level(s) for your application.

You can save your answers by clicking the pink button "Save answers". This will copy your answers in the clipboard and save a txt file containing your answers. Then you can load the answers by pasting this value in the text input below.

You can also download a csv sum up file of your answers.
